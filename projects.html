Kebele Management System

Kebele Management System is a web-based application designed to streamline the administrative processes of Kebele, the lowest administrative structure in Ethiopia. This system enables Kebele officials to efficiently store and manage resident data, post news and events, and oversee service applications. Residents can create profiles, access updates, and apply for essential services such as ID issuance, supportive letters, and certificates for marriage, birth, or death. The platform enhances transparency, accessibility, and efficiency in Kebele administration.
